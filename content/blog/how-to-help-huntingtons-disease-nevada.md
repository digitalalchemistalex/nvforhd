Huntington's Disease does not have a cure yet. But there is a lot that can be done right now — for the families living with it today.

NVforHD was built around one belief: that a community that shows up can do real, measurable good for real people. Here are five ways to be part of it.

## 1. Play Golf on May 29

The most direct way to contribute to NVforHD is to join us at the **third annual charity golf tournament at Gray's Crossing Golf Club in Truckee, CA** on May 29, 2026.

It is a four-person scramble — beginner-friendly, beautiful course, genuinely fun day. Registration includes your round of golf, on-course activities, and post-round dinner and awards.

100% of tournament proceeds go to the UC Davis Huntington's Disease Center of Excellence — the only specialty HD clinic serving 90+ Northern Nevada families.

**[Reserve your spot →](https://www.tripsee.travel/merchant/book/index.html?ref=2026NVforHDGolfTournament)**

## 2. Make a Direct Donation

You do not need to play golf. A direct donation to NVforHD goes to exactly the same place: the UC Davis HD Center of Excellence and the 90+ Northern Nevada families it serves.

There is no meaningful minimum. A $100 donation funds a portion of a patient's genetic counseling session. A $500 donation helps fund a research coordinator for a week. A $3,000 donation funds a full sponsorship level at the tournament.

Contact us at **info@nvforhd.com** or call **775-691-8860** to donate directly.

## 3. Become a Tournament Sponsor

If you own or run a business in Northern Nevada, tournament sponsorship is a meaningful way to align your brand with a cause that matters in our community.

Sponsorship levels start at $100 for hole sponsorship and go up to $3,500 for title sponsorship. Every sponsor is recognized on the course, on the NVforHD website with a backlink, and in tournament materials.

More importantly, your logo goes on a hole sign at one of the most beautiful golf courses in the Sierra Nevada — and your business becomes known as one that fights for families dealing with HD.

Email **info@nvforhd.com** to discuss sponsorship.

## 4. Volunteer at the Event

NVforHD runs on a small, passionate team. Tournament day requires extra hands for registration, on-course hole management, scoring, dinner setup, and general logistics.

If you want to be part of the day without playing, volunteer slots are available. It is a great way to experience the event, meet the community, and contribute meaningfully without a financial commitment.

Reach out at **info@nvforhd.com** to express interest.

## 5. Share This Page — Especially With HD Families

This one costs nothing and may matter more than you think.

HD is a rare disease. Families dealing with a new diagnosis often do not know:
- That a specialty clinic exists at UC Davis serving Northern Nevada
- That IVF with genetic testing can break the generational chain of HD
- That HelpCureHD provides grants to families who cannot afford it
- That NVforHD exists and hosts a tournament specifically to fund these resources

If you know someone in Northern Nevada who has an HD diagnosis in their family — share this website with them. Share the UC Davis clinic information. Share the HelpCureHD link.

Information finds families when people pass it along.
