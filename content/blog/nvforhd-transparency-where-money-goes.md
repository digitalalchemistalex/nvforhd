## The Model

NVforHD is a Nevada-based non-profit. Every year we host one charity golf tournament. Every dollar raised goes to one chosen cause — selected specifically because it creates maximum direct impact on HD patients and families in our region.

We do not split proceeds across multiple organisations. We do not take an administrative percentage. We do not have paid staff drawing salaries from the tournament revenue.

The model is intentionally simple: **raise it, give it, prove it.**

:::fact NVforHD is a registered Nevada non-profit organisation. Donations may be tax-deductible — consult your tax advisor for details specific to your situation.

## 2024: HelpCureHD and the Puccini Family

**Amount raised: $25,000**
**Beneficiary: HelpCureHD**
**Purpose: IVF with preimplantation genetic testing for one HD family**

:::image /images/rb-letter.png | Thank-you letter from the Puccini family — proof of direct impact | The letter that arrived after the 2024 tournament

The 2024 inaugural tournament at Old Greenwood Golf Club in Truckee sold out. $25,000 was raised and transferred in full to HelpCureHD — a non-profit founded by MLB pitcher Joe Smith and TNT reporter Allie LaForce after Joe's mother Lee was diagnosed with HD and passed away at 61.

HelpCureHD went through their grant cycle and selected Brandon and Rylee Puccini — a young Northern Nevada couple carrying the HD gene who could not afford IVF.

Rylee is now pregnant. Their baby will not have Huntington's Disease.

:::pull "One golf tournament. One day. One family's next generation freed from this disease. That is the proof of concept."

**What the $25,000 paid for:**
- IVF cycle including egg retrieval and fertilisation
- Preimplantation genetic testing (PGT-M) to identify HD-free embryos
- Embryo transfer procedure
- Initial monitoring and support

## 2025: UC Davis HD Center of Excellence

**Amount raised: Proceeds from 2025 tournament**
**Beneficiary: UC Davis Huntington's Disease Center of Excellence, Sacramento CA**
**Purpose: Operational funding for Northern Nevada's only HD specialist clinic**

In 2025, NVforHD shifted focus to the 90+ Northern Nevada families already living with HD — families who need ongoing specialist care, not just a one-time intervention.

The UC Davis HD Center of Excellence is the closest HDSA-designated HD specialist clinic to Reno. Families drive 130 miles to Sacramento because it is the only option within reach.

The clinic operates entirely on private donation. It employs:

- 2 Movement Disorder Neurologists specialising in HD
- 2 Psychiatrists with specialised HD training
- 1 HD Genetic Counselor
- 1 HD Social Worker
- 2 Physical Therapists with HD experience
- 3 Research Coordinators

:::fact UC Davis is one of only 48 HDSA Centers of Excellence in the United States. It is the closest to Northern Nevada. Source: HDSA Center of Excellence Program

## How Your Money Moves

1. You register for the tournament or donate directly
2. NVforHD collects all tournament and donation revenue
3. At the close of the tournament cycle, 100% of net proceeds are transferred to the designated cause
4. NVforHD publishes the final amount raised on the website

There is no step where money is diverted, held, or skimmed.
